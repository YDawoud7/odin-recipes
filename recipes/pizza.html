Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients
  2. Allow your dough to rest on the counter for about 5 minutes
  3. Rub your surface (and your hands) with a few tablespoons of olive oil
  4. You have a few options when it comes to shaping the dough. Stretch it in the air, use a rolling pin, or pat it with your hands
  5. Make sure not to overwork the dough
